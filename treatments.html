Treatments & Specialities

Dr. Samit Chaturvedi offers advanced urology care with special focus on robotic urology, uro-oncology, renal transplant, laser urology, stone disease, prostate care, laparoscopic urology, and reconstructive urology.

Robotic Urology
Robotic urology uses advanced robotic technology to perform complex urological surgeries with precision. Dr. Samit Chaturvedi performs robotic procedures for prostate, kidney, bladder, ureter, transplant, and reconstructive urological conditions.
Procedures Offered
  • Robotic Radical Prostatectomy
  • Robotic Partial Nephrectomy
  • Robotic Radical Nephrectomy
  • Robotic Nephro-Ureterectomy
  • Robotic Radical Cystectomy
  • Robotic Pyeloplasty
  • Robotic Ureteric Reimplantation
  • Robotic VVF Repair
  • Robotic Renal Transplant
  • Robotic RPLND
  • Boari Flap Reconstruction
Uro-Oncology
Uro-oncology focuses on cancers of the urinary system and male reproductive organs. Dr. Chaturvedi has special expertise in robotic and minimally invasive cancer surgeries.

  • Testicular Cancer

  • Partial Nephrectomy

  • Lymph Node Dissection

Laser Urology

Reconstructive Urology
Reconstructive urology helps restore normal urinary function in patients with urethral stricture, bladder problems, fistula, or ureteric injury.

  • Buccal Mucosal Graft Urethroplasty
  • Anastomotic Urethroplasty
  • Pyeloplasty
  • Bladder Augmentation
  • VVF Repair
  • UVF Repair
  • Ureteric Reconstruction
  • Boari Flap Reconstruction
Laparoscopic Urology
