Senior Urologist & Robotic Surgeon

Dr. Samit Chaturvedi

Director – Robotic Urology, Uro-Oncology & Renal Transplant, KIIMS Hyderabad

Dr. Samit Chaturvedi is a highly experienced urologist and robotic surgeon. He is currently Director of Robotic Urology, Uro-Oncology and Renal Transplant at KIIMS Institute of Urological Sciences, Kondapur, Hyderabad.

He specializes in robotic surgery, uro-oncology, renal transplant, laser urology, laparoscopic urology, endourology, and reconstructive urology. His work includes robotic prostate surgery, robotic kidney surgery, robotic bladder surgery, robotic renal transplant, kidney stone treatment, prostate enlargement treatment, and urethral reconstruction.

Experience

Dr. Chaturvedi has worked at leading hospitals including Holy Family Hospital, SGPGI Lucknow, BLK Hospital, PSRI Hospital, Max Hospital Saket, and KIIMS Hospital Hyderabad. He has served in senior roles including Consultant, Senior Consultant, Principal Consultant, Associate Director, and Director in Urology, Robotic Surgery, Renal Transplant, and Uro-Oncology.
